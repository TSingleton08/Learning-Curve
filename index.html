In this video body cam footage shows suspect who flees from police leads to officer involved shooting. Body cam footage captures a traffic stop in Milwaukee where the front passenger of the vehicle lied to the police. When the male passenger was told to exit the vehicle, he managed to climb into the drivers seat and drive away in vehicle, as he knew he had outstanding warrant(s). The officers attempt to catch up to the vehicle, but lost it in traffic. The officers later located the suspect who was hiding in the back of another occupied vehicle. As officers attempted to secure the suspect they learned he was armed with handgun. At that point, an officer involved shooting occurred.
